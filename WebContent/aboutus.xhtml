About yourhotdeal.com

Founded in 2010, yourhotdeal.com is a free, user-driven deal sharing site with a mission to provide consumers an avenue to collaborate and share information in order to make the best shopping decisions. Yourhotdeal achieves this by providing its users a forum for communication and various shopping tools. yourhotdeal.com prides itself on being user and community focused, never allowing paid placement for frontpage deal listings.
