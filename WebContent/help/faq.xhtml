  • I just finished a purchase. Why isn’t it showing up in my account?

    Cash-back rebates do not appear immediately in your account. Each day, yourHotDeal receives reports from merchants listing qualified transactions (ie, purchases that are eligible for cash back). Once we receive and process this data, the order will appear in your account.

  • How long before the Cash back appears in my account?

    Reporting time varies by merchant. Generally, your cash back rebate will appear within a few days of your transaction. However, some stores will not report a transaction until it has shipped out to the customer. Some of our travel merchants will not report transactions until the reservation has been completed.

  • Okay, it has been 30 days and my rebate has still not reported. Now what?

    If your cash back rebate still has not appeared, you can to go to your account -> order inquery to submit order inquery. Our customer service agent will process them and may open a claim with merchants who allow a claim process. All claims are subject to the Terms and Conditions that all members agree to when joining yourHotDeal.

  • What is the difference between "Pending Cash back" and "Available Cash back"?

    "Pending Cash Back" are initially posted to your account upon initial receipt of an order from one of our partner stores. Pending Cash Back stay in a "Pending" state for 60 days (based on the "Posting Date") to account for different store return policies, order cancellations and to provide ample time for collection of commissions from stores. Once Pending Cash Back have moved to Available status, those cash back can be "cashed in" by requesting a payment via Paypal. Please note that you must meet the minimum amount of Available Rebates needed to request payment which is $25.

  • Why is there a wait for "Pending Cash Back" to move to "Available"?

    Since most merchants have a period when returns and exchanges are allowed, we must provide a similar time frame, in which merchants can adjust rebates when orders are changed. Please note, merchants have different return and cancellation policies. 60 days allows us to cover all of our merchants’ time limits for returns or changes to an order.

  • It has been 60 days and my Cash Back is still "Pending". What should I do?

    Please contact Customer Support and we will assist you further. However, please remember that the 60-day period begins on the date the transaction posts to your account, not the date that the order takes place.

  • When will my Cash Back become "Available"?

    Cash back are automatically moved into "Available" status when we collect the cash back from the store. Usually it takes 60 days!

Eligibility

  • Are purchases of gift cards eligible for cash back cash back?

    Some stores do offer cash back for gift card purchases as listed in the Gift Cards category. However, any store not listed in that "Gift Cards" category do not offer cash back for gift card purchases.

  • What if no link is found for the product I want to buy?

    As always, you can go to page, find the store you want to buy from and click shop to go to store page and finish your purchase

  • Will I get a cash back if I pay with a gift card?

    Probably not. Whether you use it to pay for the whole order or just part of one, a gift card generally voids cash back on your purchases.

  • Can I check out other websites after clicking through yourHotDeal?

    We understand that comparison shopping is important. However, surfing away from the yourHotDeal site during a transaction may cause tracking problems for your transactions. This can result in cash back not posting to your account. We suggest that once you are done comparison shopping, you return to yourHotDeal and click through to the merchant one more time and make your final purchase. In comparison shop, but I just open new browser tabs. That’s okay, right? Unfortunately, no. Having too many windows or tabs open can confuse tracking systems and award the commission to another website. Again, we suggest that you finish comparison shopping, close all other windows or tabs, and then click through yourHotDeal one last time for your final purchase.

  • Can I get a cash back for a pre-order?

    Pre-Orders are not suggested if the cash back tracking is important to you as pre-orders will likely have issues with cash back.

  • If I purchase a subscription, will I get cash back each time it renews?

    Unless explicitly stated, subscriptions come with a one-time rebate. Auto-renewals are not eligible for cash back. This is also true of auto-delivery or "auto-ship" programs.

  • Can I use more than one cash back program for a purchase?

    No, yourHotDeal cannot be combined with other cash back programs or rewards sites.

  • Are taxes, service fees or shipping eligible for cash back?

    No, taxes, along with any fees charged by the merchant, are not eligible for cash back.

  • Can I get cash back on warranties?

    Extended warranties that are sold along with merchandise are not eligible for cash back. This includes plans such as travel insurance.

  • I don't live in the U.S. or Canada. Will I get cash back on my purchases?

    Most international orders are eligible for cash back. However, we cannot guarantee that you will receive cash back for your purchases due to some of the internal merchant policies with international orders.

  • Can I use a coupon that I found outside of yourHotDeal?

    No, most coupon codes you find outside of yourHotDeal will void cash back on your purchases or negate the cash back tracking. With that being said, there are some merchants that may allow the "stacking" of coupons and cash back but we certainly advise our customers that using coupons from outside sources will void the cash back per our Terms and Conditions. Your best bet is to use any coupon displayed at yourHotDeal as those coupons are specifically approved for use with yourHotDeal and will not affect cash back.

  • Can I use a coupon displayed on the merchant’s website?

    If the offer is visible on the merchant's website then it should not interfere with your cash back.

  • What about coupons that the merchant sends me directly?

    These are usually not eligible for rebates. Be sure to check out our coupons for the merchant though. We may have a similar (or an even better) deal that will get you cash back in addition to approved coupon savings.

  • A coupon from yourHotDeal is not working correctly. What should I do?

    Please Contact Us so that we can test the coupon ourselves and determine if the discount is expired or if there are restrictions that may be causing issue with the coupon.

  • After I checked out, I noticed your coupon didn’t work. Will you credit my account?

    yourHotDeal offers coupons as a courtesy to customers so that you can get the best deal possible. However, yourHotDeal has no control over how a merchant applies or honors the coupons. As such, it is your responsibility be sure the coupon has been accepted before you check out. If the discount is important to you, we can suggest that you cancel the order and begin a new one.

  • I want to place multiple orders at a store. What is the best way to insure cash back tracking?

    If you are placing multiple orders at a store, you will need to have a unique click-through to that store from yourHotDeal for each unique order. I.E. Place your first order at the store through a yourHotDeal' store link, come back to yourHotDeal, click-through to the store again and place your second order, etc.
