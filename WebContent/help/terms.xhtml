Earning Cash Back

Earning back a portion of your purchases as cash back ("Cash Back") is straightforward. Choose a store, product, coupon or other offer displayed on YourHotDeal.com or an YourHotDeal application, click the accompanying shopping link to be directed to an affiliated store's website ("Affiliate Store"), and complete a purchase to earn Cash Back on your net purchase amount. Please note that the net purchase amount excludes taxes, fees, shipping, gift-wrapping, discounts or creYourHotDealreturns or cancellations, and extended warranties.

Certain Affiliate Stores exclude a limited number of products and purchases from the Program. YourHotDeal does its best to minimize and maintain an up-to-date list of exclusions on YourHotDeal.com. Additionally, to earn Cash Back, you must complete your purchase during the same shopping session you start after clicking on the YourHotDeal link. If you visit other sites before completing your purchase or use coupons not provided by YourHotDeal, your purchase might be associated with a service other than YourHotDeal and you might not earn cash back on your purchase. If you disable 'cookies' on your computer you will not be able to earn Cash Back as cookies are used to authenticate the user and be certain Cash Back is assigned to the Member account.
